Aussie Dave

David "Aussie Dave" Griffiths

Dave grew up in a small country town in regional Australia, but always wanted to see the world. This thirst for adventure led to cruise ships where he spent 5 years traveling the world where he visited 48 countries on 6 continents. He worked as an Entertainment Host, DJ and Cruise Director and eventually settled in Orlando, Florida. As one of the first Elite Beats employees he has worked on most major endeavors of the company!



Robbie G

Robbie "Robbie G" Gomez

Robbie is a DJ, Emcee, Actor and Stilt Walker that specializes in family friendly, high energy interactive entertainment. He graduated with a degree in Theatre Performance and Management from the University of South Florida. When not performing, Robbie spends his time flying all over the country as a commercial pilot.



DJ Jayce

Jason "DJ Jayce" Gabel

DJ Jayce, born and raised in New York, has been developing his craft of entertaining and captivating audiences from coast to coast over the last 25 years. His passion, attention to detail and professionalism is like having additional event insurance. Like a chameleon, he is able to adapt to each event and the audience he is working with to create an atmosphere that will make you look like a hero. Over the last 14 years, he has been an unstoppable force in the corporate and social markets.
